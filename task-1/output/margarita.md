# Cocktail Recipes

## Margarita

![Margarita](https://www.thecocktaildb.com/images/media/drink/5noda61589575158.jpg/medium)

**Category**: Ordinary Drink

**Alcoholic**: Yes

### Ingredients

- 1 1/2 oz Tequila
- 1/2 oz Triple sec
- 1 oz Lime juice
- Salt

### Instructions

Rub the rim of the glass.

Serve in: Cocktail glass
